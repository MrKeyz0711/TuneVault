**🎵 OffBeats**

Your music. Your device. No strings attached.
Tired of sitting through ads every two minutes because you won't pay for premium? Same. OffBeats was born out of that frustration, plus a love of building playlists in my college radio days. So I built my own player witht the help of AI. And now it's yours too.

**🚀 Zero Setup. Seriously.**

Visit the GitHub Pages site, or just download the single HTML file and open it in any browser. No installation. No account. No nonsense. You're listening in seconds with your own tracks.

**🎧 Your Library, Your Rules**

Drop in any MP3 or WAV file — songs, sound effects, podcast clips, whatever you've got. OffBeats stores everything directly on your device, which means:

✈️ **No WiFi?** No problem. Your music works at 30,000 feet.

🏕️ **Middle of nowhere?** Still works.

📵** No signal?** Doesn't matter.

Streaming platforms hide offline listening behind a paywall. OffBeats doesn't charge a dime. Your files are already there, ready to go.

**📦 Take It With You**

Hit Export and your entire library gets bundled into a single ZIP file; dropped right into your downloads. Transfer it to another device, send it to a friend, or just keep it as a backup. Hit Import on the other end and everything loads right back in, good as new.

**📱 Install It Like an App**

OffBeats is a PWA; a Progressive Web App. Fancy way of saying: open it in your phone's browser, tap "Add to Home Screen," and boom. It lives on your home screen like any other app, on both iPhone and Android. No App Store. No Play Store. Just tap and play.

**✅ Features**

🎵 Unlimited MP3 / WAV storage
💾 Automatic local storage- your library never disappears
🔍 Search bar for when your collection gets huge
⏯️ Full playback controls— play, pause, skip, loop
📦 One-click import & export as a ZIP
📱 Installable on iPhone and Android as a home screen app



**🔒 No Accounts. No Logins. No Servers.**

Everything stays on your device. There's no backend, no database, no login server. Just you and your music. The way it should be.
